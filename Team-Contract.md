# Digital Dynamos — Team Contract

> Converted from the signed PDF team contract for version-control friendliness.

## 1. Overall Objectives

The objective of this team is to design and develop a Human Motion Analysis system that analyzes African dance movements using a webcam or Xbox 360/One Kinect device. The team aims to deliver a functional, well-documented software solution that demonstrates applied computer science principles, teamwork, and professional software development practices. The project will culminate in a successful presentation to the advisor and stakeholders.

## 3. Values and Agreement Statements

1. All team members agree to communicate respectfully, professionally, and transparently.  
2. All members commit to completing assigned tasks on time and notifying the team early if issues arise.  
3. The team agrees to collaborate openly, value feedback, and support one another to achieve project goals.  

## 4. Software Configuration Management Protocol

- The team will use GitHub for version control and collaboration.  
- All code will be written in Python.  
- All development will occur in feature branches.  
- Changes will be merged into the `main` branch only through pull requests.  
- At least one team member must review and approve each pull request before merging.  
- Clear and descriptive commit messages are required.  
- Merge conflicts will be resolved collaboratively by affected team members to maintain code quality and consistency.

## 6. Meeting Dates/Times With Advisor

## 7. Communication

The team will communicate using the following platforms:

## 8. Conflict Resolution Protocol

1. Address concerns directly and respectfully with the involved team member.  
2. If unresolved, discuss the issue as a team during a scheduled meeting.  
3. If the conflict persists, escalate the issue to the advisor for guidance and resolution.  

## 9. Consequences

- Failure to meet responsibilities may result in reassignment of tasks.  
- Repeated violations will be documented and discussed with the team.  
- Continued non-compliance may be reported to the advisor and may impact individual evaluations or grades.
